Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our pricing and services.

What's included in the page count?

One page equals 275-300 words with standard formatting (12pt font, double spacing, 1-inch margins). We charge based on the actual word count of your completed document.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, our pricing is completely transparent. The price you see is what you pay, with no hidden charges. Any additional services (like urgent delivery) will be clearly communicated and agreed upon beforehand.

How do you ensure the quality of your work?

Every document goes through our rigorous quality assurance process, including plagiarism checks, editing, and proofreading by our expert team. We also offer free revisions to ensure your complete satisfaction.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Payment is required upfront for new clients, with a 50% deposit for large projects over $500.

Can I get a discount for recurring orders?

Yes! We offer loyalty discounts for returning clients. After your first order, you'll automatically qualify for a 5% discount on all future orders, increasing up to 15% for frequent clients.
